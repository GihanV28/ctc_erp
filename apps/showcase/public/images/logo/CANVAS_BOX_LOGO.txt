CANVAS BOX LOGO
================

The footer now includes a "Powered by Canvas Box" section.

The logo should be placed here as:
- Filename: tcb.png
- Recommended size: 200x50px or similar
- Format: PNG with transparency preferred
- Should be a horizontal logo that works on dark backgrounds

If the image file is not found, it will fallback to showing "Canvas Box" as text.

The logo is displayed with:
- Opacity: 70% normally, 100% on hover
- Height: 16px (h-4 class)
- Auto width to maintain aspect ratio
